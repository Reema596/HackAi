About Us

Making a difference, one meal at a time.

Our Mission

At Food Donation, we strive to eliminate hunger by reducing food waste. We connect surplus food with people in need, ensuring that no meal goes to waste.

How We Work

1. Food Collection

We collect surplus food from restaurants, supermarkets, and local donors, ensuring that perfectly good food is redirected to NGOs instead of being wasted.

2. Quality Check

Our team carefully inspects and sorts donated food to ensure it meets safety and hygiene standards before being handed over to NGOs for distribution.

3. Distribution

We collaborate with trusted NGOs to distribute food efficiently, ensuring that meals reach to those who needs most.

4. Impact Tracking

We carefully monitor and track every food donation from collection to distribution, ensuring it reaches those who need it most.

Why Food Donation Matters

Every year, 1.3 billion tons of food is wasted while millions go hungry. Our platform bridges this gap.
